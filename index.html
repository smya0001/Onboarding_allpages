Welcome to Sunrise Parry!

What is Sunrise Parry?

Sunrise Parry is a website that allows users living within Victoria to have access to various functions that provides sun safe practices.

Within UV Level, we provide a function for users to view the UV level of the user's current location.

Within UV Trend, we provide a function for users to view the data regarding UV trends and how uv may impact the skin.

Within Protection, we provide two main functions. To provide information on apropriate clothings depending on the level of uv level and a calculator for the right sunscreen level needed.

What is the purpose of this site?

Australia has some of the world's highest levels of ultraviolet radiation. Victorian’s, especially children, already face the world’s highest UV exposure due to geographic location.

Combined with the soaring popularity of outdoor activities, children are facing alarming risks from sun exposure. With the increase of skin cancer, there is a need to raise awareness to parents on how we might help parents to safeguard children against the sun’s harmful rays to prevent skin damage.

The goal of this website is to provide these people with the self-assurance and tools they need to start and maintain sun protection routines.
